1. Understanding the Stock Market

The stock market is where investors buy and sell shares of publicly traded companies. Stocks represent ownership in a company. When a company does well, its stock price generally rises, and shareholders benefit.

Markets are influenced by economic trends, earnings reports, interest rates, news events, and investor psychology.
